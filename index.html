Ever since I was a child, I have been obsessed with storytelling. Growing up as a Greek-American I lived and breathed the mythological stories of my ancestors. These stories only furthered my imagination and obsession around how people and companies tell, shape, and sell their stories. I couldn’t be more thrilled to have a career where I help businesses and brands build, shape and tell their stories through design.

My focus on research and strategy makes my design solutions more efficient, delightful and elegant for both businesses and their users alike. I thrive in environments that are research and data heavy, so I can craft strategies that are purposeful and deliberate. I build design systems that solve problems for products and brands.
